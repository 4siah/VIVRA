01.

Services and Facilities

What services are included?

All our residences include WiFi, cleaning of common areas, 24/7 security, and access to study rooms. Some locations also offer gym and laundry facilities.

Are utilities included in the rent?

Yes, water, electricity, heating, and internet are included in the monthly rent. No surprise bills or additional costs.

02.

Reservation Process

How do I make a reservation?

You can reserve online through our website or contact our team directly. A deposit is required to secure your room.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid ID or passport, proof of enrollment or acceptance letter, and a completed application form.

03.

Moving and Daily Life

When can I move in?

Move-in dates are flexible. We'll coordinate with you to find a suitable date and time. Our team will be there to welcome you.

Can I receive visitors?

Yes, visitors are welcome during designated hours. Overnight guests may be allowed with prior notice, depending on the residence policy.

04.

Stays and Policies

Can I stay for just one semester or during the summer?

Of course. We offer semester-long stays and summer stays. Check availability with our team.

What happens if I want to cancel my contract?

Cancellations are accepted if justified: non-admission to university (first choice), visa refusal, failure to pass university entrance exams, or waiting for final grades (depending on availability). Cancellations must be notified by email as soon as possible and refunds will be processed within 30 days of notification.
